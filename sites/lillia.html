Lillia Jungle Tips and Tricks

  • Lillia is a scaling champion which means that she has to avoid early game duels with the enemy jungler and instead focus on farming & looking for free kills from ganks.

  • Prance stacks from Blooming Blows allows Lillia to move a lot faster due to the huge amounts of bonus movement speed these provide. Use this to your advantage and prioritize ganking a lane which has camps nearby so that you can rush into a gank with full speed.

  • Lillia's combo goes as follows: Blooming Blows, Lilting Lullaby, Swirlseed, Watch out! Eep! and keep kiting them with Blooming Blows until they're dead.

  • Swirlseed is a great spell to try and get a free kill as you can cast it off lane. If it hits a champion, sleep them with Lilting Lullaby so that your allies can finish them off. It can also be used for engaging, but when it comes to team fights it is much better to Flash into a group of enemies, Blooming Blows them for the AoE and sleep them all with Lilting Lullaby.
