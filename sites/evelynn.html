Evelynn Jungle Tips and Tricks

  • Evelynn is able to know when she has been spotted due to the glowing yellow eye over nearby enemy champions and changes to her stealth circle.

  • Every Evelynn player will want to focus on power farming until level six as that is when Evelynn gains her much needed ultimate and the previously mentioned stealth mechanic. Evelynn's early game is very weak, and her gank capabilities are close to non-existent unless the enemy is heavily over-extending.

  • Evelynn is very simple to gank with— utilize her stealth to get close to the enemy and cast Allure & wait for the heart to fill up. Cast Hate Spike as this will charm the enemy to walk towards you, keep spamming Hate Spike while also using Whiplash to deal tremendous amounts of damage. Most enemies at this point have very low life, and this is when Evelynn can finish off the enemy with ease by using her ultimate Last Caress.

  • Last Caress does bonus damage to Baron and Dragon when they are below 30% health, this allows Evelynn to secure objectives with ease by timing Last Caress and Smite properly.
