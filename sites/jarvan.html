Jarvan IV Jungle Tips and Tricks

  • Jarvan IV is very effective for climbing in ranked as his spell kit allows him to do pretty much everything a jungler would need to do. He can do early game ganks, duel most enemy junglers, take early Herald/Dragons and most importantly he's very mobile.

  • Jarvan IV has strong early-game ganking potential. After taking your first buff and reaching level 2, consider ganking a nearby lane with your E-Q combo. It can catch opponents off guard and potentially secure an early kill.

  • Martial Cadence deals a substantial amount of bonus damage, but it has a cooldown! When you're fighting multiple enemies try to proc it on each one for the highest damage output.

  • The Demacian Standard + Dragon Strike combo can be used to initiate fights, but also to run away from them if needed.

  • Cataclysm is incredibly useful for teamfights as it allows Jarvan IV to trap immobile enemies and should almost always be used on carries (ADC/MID).
